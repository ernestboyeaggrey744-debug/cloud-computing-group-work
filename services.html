We Offer

Comprehensive support and solutions to empower Ghanaian shippers

Policy Advocacy

Representing and protecting the interests of shippers at national and international levels to ensure fair and efficient trade practices.

Freight Cost Monitoring

Tracking and analyzing freight charges to help shippers make informed decisions and negotiate better rates.

Shipper Complaint Handling

Providing a structured mechanism for addressing operational challenges and disputes faced by shippers at ports and terminals.

Training & Capacity Building

Organizing seminars, workshops, and training programs to build the capacity of shippers on logistics, documentation, and compliance.

Trade Facilitation

Supporting shippers with information and services that simplify international trade and improve cargo movement efficiency.

Stakeholder Engagement

Collaborating with shipping lines, port operators, and regulatory bodies to create a smooth, transparent, and competitive shipping environment.
